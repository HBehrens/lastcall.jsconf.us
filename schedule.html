Saturday December 5th, 2015

Conference Tracks

What is Track A

Traditionally a two track conference format, JSConf US provides a track of curated set of speakers as outlined here that have submitted the proposal and successfully made it through the selection process.

What is Track B

The alternative track for JSConf US is driven by people like you! We have a very unique format for our second track in that we let anyone register to speak in a first-come, first-speaking format. These talks are generally from the full range of node.js development and are some of the most exciting talks at JSConf. Registration opens about one month before the event.
